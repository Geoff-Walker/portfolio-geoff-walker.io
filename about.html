About Me

Hi my names Geoff and I live in rural Lincolnshire with my beautiful wife Helen. After a nasty fall left me an amputee, my established career as an engineer ended abruptly, little did I know when I started coding as a hobby that my true calling for programming had only just begun.

Since then I have fallen in love with programming and developed a talent for interactive UI/UX web development. If you need help with your project on a freelance basis or would like to discuss a more permanent post please get in touch.
